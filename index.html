The Idea

Wine doesn't need to feel complicated to be good.

Most of the time the right bottle is simply the one that feels right for the table — something fresh, expressive, and made by people who care about what they're doing.

Thirsty Cunt is a brand for the curious — people who order wine often, host the dinner, travel for the bottle. People who want in on the conversation without the lecture.
