2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use the App:

Information you provide

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Quebec's Law 25, we process your data on the following bases:

  • Legitimate interest: to improve our services and ensure the App's security

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App's services.

If you delete your account, your personal information will be erased within 30 days, except for data we are required to retain under applicable law.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or alteration, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)

  • Continuous system monitoring

8. Your Rights

Depending on your place of residence, you have certain rights regarding your personal data:

Rights under Quebec's Law 25 and the GDPR (Europe)

  • Right of access: obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: request the deletion of your data
  • Right to portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object: object to certain processing of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent: at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
  • Right to file a complaint: with the Commission d'accès à l'information du Québec (CAI) or the data protection authority of your country

Rights under CCPA / CPRA (California)

  • Right to know: what personal data is collected, used, and shared
  • Right to deletion: request the deletion of your personal data
  • Right to opt out of sale: we do not sell your personal data
  • Right to non-discrimination: you will not be penalized for exercising your rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at dev@thirstyc.com. We will respond to your request within the timeframes required by applicable law.
